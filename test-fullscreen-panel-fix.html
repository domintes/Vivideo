Problem: Vivideo panel was invisible in fullscreen mode, unlike other extensions like Video Speed Controller.

Solution: Move panel to fullscreen element during fullscreen mode to ensure visibility.

üî¨ Research Findings

Why other extensions work: Extensions like Video Speed Controller move their UI elements to the fullscreen video element, making them visible within the fullscreen context.

Technical Solution:

  • Monitor fullscreen events (standard + webkit/moz/ms)
  • Move Vivideo panel to fullscreen element when entering fullscreen
  • Restore panel to original position when exiting fullscreen
  • Enhanced CSS z-index and positioning for better visibility
  • Fallback positioning if panel movement fails
